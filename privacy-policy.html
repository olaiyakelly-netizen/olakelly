Privacy Policy

A simple explanation of what this site collects, why it is collected, and how it is handled.

Last updated March 20, 2026

Introduction

Ola Kelly is a content-based website focused on leadership, stewardship, and thoughtful decision-making. This policy explains, in plain language, what information may be collected when you visit the site or subscribe to the newsletter.

This policy is intentionally simple. It reflects what the site actually does.

Information We Collect

If you subscribe to the newsletter, the only personal information collected is your email address. Newsletter subscriptions are managed through TinyEmail.

The site collects limited usage data such as page views, browser type, and device type through basic analytics tools. This information is used in aggregate to understand how the site is being used.

How We Use Information

Email addresses are used to send newsletter updates, essays, and occasional site-related communication you have chosen to receive.

Basic usage data is used to improve the site, understand what content is most useful, and maintain performance.

Data Sharing

Your information is never sold.

Information may be shared only with service providers that help operate the site, such as TinyEmail for newsletter delivery or analytics providers used to understand site traffic. Those providers only receive the information needed to perform their role.

Data Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect the information collected through this site. No online system can promise absolute security, but care is taken to work with reputable service providers and to limit collection to what is actually needed.

Your Rights

You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email.

You can also request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information by emailing privacy@olakelly.com.

Contact Information

For privacy questions or requests, contact:

privacy@olakelly.com
